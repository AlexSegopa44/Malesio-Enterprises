Who We Are

More Than
Logistics

Malesio Enterprises (Pty) Ltd was founded with a clear purpose — to provide businesses and individuals across Gauteng with logistics they can genuinely rely on. We started with a single truck and a commitment to showing up, every time.

Today we operate across removals, supply chain management, transportation, freight forwarding, and fuel supply. What hasn't changed is the work ethic behind every delivery and the personal accountability we hold ourselves to.

We're not a faceless corporation. We're a Gauteng-based team that answers the phone, meets deadlines, and treats every client's cargo like it's our own.
